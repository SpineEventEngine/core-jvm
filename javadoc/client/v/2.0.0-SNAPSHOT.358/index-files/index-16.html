an acknowledgement. The received `Ack` may have one of the following statuses:
   1. `Status.ok`  the command is accepted for further processing.
   2. `Status.error`  there has been an error receiving the command. No further
       processing would occur.
   3. `Status.rejection`  business rules do not allow the execution of the command.
       E.g., a user who made a request do not have the required permission.

would not be received. All errors which occur on the side of gRPC should
be handled by the means of the chosen client stub.
